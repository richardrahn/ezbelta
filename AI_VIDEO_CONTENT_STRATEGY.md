### Common Limitations & Complaints

#### 1. Authenticity Issues

**Problem:**
- Unrealistic facial expressions and limited emotional range
- Robotic appearance and "uncanny valley" effect
- Artificial lip-sync gaps and timing issues
- No hand tracking on most platforms
- Limited body language

**Impact:**
- May feel less authentic than human presenters
- Not ideal for emotional or sensitive topics
- Some audiences may reject AI-generated content

**Mitigation:**
- Choose platforms with best lip-sync (HeyGen rated highest)
- Use for educational/informational content (not emotional appeals)
- Mix AI avatars with real footage (hybrid approach)
- Be transparent: "This video was created with AI to bring you content faster"

---

#### 2. Cost Concerns

**Problem:**
- Credit-based systems limit usage (D-ID, others)
- Hidden costs (HD exports, custom avatars, language packs)
- High enterprise pricing (Synthesia, Hour One)
- Per-minute restrictions reduce value
- Add-ons and upgrades increase total cost

**Impact:**
- Budget unpredictability
- May need to upgrade tiers faster than expected
- ROI diminishes with add-on costs

**Mitigation:**
- Choose unlimited plans (HeyGen) to avoid credit anxiety
